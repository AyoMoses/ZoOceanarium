WHO WE ARE

zoOceanarium Group specializes in developing and operating a new generation of aquarium and zoological facilities that emphasize excellent animal care and wellbeing and that create unique opportunities for people to appreciate and learn about animals through once-in-a-lifetime experiences.

By providing all areas of management for all types of animal programs including wildlife-focused science centers, public aquariums, and zoological parks, the company currently works closely with all types of businesses including developers, design firms, and existing aquariums and zoos.

AT YOUR SERVICE, ANYWHERE IN THE WORLD.

zoOceanarium Group has a wide range of resources positioned globally, with clients in more than 10 countries around the world.

ABOUT US

COMPANY BACKGROUND

zoOceanarium Group started as a small company with a handful of dedicated staff. Over just a few years, it has grown into one of the largest companies in the world of its kind. With each of the projects that the company is involved with, the goal is to create fun and educational experiences through a combination of components that revolve around interactively and hands-on experiential learning. The team's overarching interest is in creating a crossroad between science centers and animal attractions.

zoOceanarium Group is well qualified and situated to quickly take on any project. The company has been involved in projects in dozens of countries on four different continents. Everything from remote consulting on design and operational matters to full scale operations of all areas of the facility. Most notable for the company’s experience is The Green Planet in Dubai which is a tropical rainforest themed biodome, St. Louis Aquarium which is a public aquarium with visitation over 1 million annually, RWS Marine Life Park in Singapore with a development budget of over half a billion dollars, and Sea World Geoje in S. Korea.
